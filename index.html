I’m Ayush Devkota, a passionate web and UI/UX designer focused on creating clean, user-friendly, and visually engaging digital experiences. I enjoy turning ideas into functional designs that not only look good but also solve real problems.

Currently, I’m building my skills in web design and development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, while continuously exploring modern design trends and tools like Figma. I’m always eager to learn, improve, and take on new challenges that push my creativity.

My goal is to grow as a designer and work on projects that make a meaningful impact on users’ lives.
